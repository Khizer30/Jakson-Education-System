Jakson Education System is one of the emerging educational institutions of Pakistan. It portrays the unique concept of “School Without Bag” with “Hands-on Learning” phenomenon. Jakson Education System provides the quality education from early years till grade V. Compliant with national policies; our education system adopts analytical approach to seek the hidden talents of the future of this nation. It is our primary responsibility to make the students well equipped with the competitive knowledge and build them morally and ethically to let them contribute towards nation building.
