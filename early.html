Early Years Program

Well-trained and Montessori diploma holder tutors are our resources to train the early year students. This is the time to build the foundation of children and JES provides the best suited staff accessible to the students during the school hours.

Methodology

The program includes Pre-Nursery and Nursery students. The students are taught through activities and games, thereafter, developing their interest and provoking their thoughts.
The children learn best in supportive relationship with teachers and head of the school. They learn best through 3E’s approach i.e. Experience, Extension and Examination. The children learn in a best way when curriculum matches their culture and values and JES strives to incorporate all the approaches to teach students.

Informal Assessments

Assessment is a continuous process that takes place in all areas of school life. Through on-going assessment, we hope to build a complete picture of a student’s development, not only intellectually, but emotionally, socially and physically.
Assessment can take many forms. We assess through discussion and observation as well as using more formal procedures such as testing.
