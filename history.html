History

Jakson Education System is a concept of M/s Axiom’s Educational Wing, where the latter was comprised of the training and development of educational personnel. It is the then thought of the Chairman to incorporate the conceptual learning from the very beginning of educational life of student. AES has organized the Hands-on training similar to World Bank training format throughout the leading universities of the country and faced problems to deliver due to the lack of students’ conceptual level and competence.
The issue was because of their traditional educational system which lacks the integration of real-world examples and trainings with the routine studies. At that particular time, the AES thinktank thought of incorporating the conceptual and project-based learning from early years in schools. Hence, they transformed the idea into reality with the name of “Jakson Education System”. Chairman holds an Asia record of training to the blind students as well.
Jakson Education system is that seed planted by the C.E.O of AES based Hands-on learning rather than conventional cramming system.
