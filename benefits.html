Franchise Benefits

Work for Yourself

Are you tired working for others? Be your own boss and work for yourself. Start your own school.
Do you want to start your future as educationist or want to excel in the education sector? JES is exact choice for you to start your career in education sector. JES will provide guide lines and will help you to establish a successful school system.

Low Investment

Being a JES franchisee you don’t need huge amounts to start. With low start-up costs and best practices to achieve quick rate of profitability (ROI) JES will provide road-maps, plans, strategies and world successful examples in enterprise world. JES will guide and provide services from planning a school to successfully maintaining the institution.

Innovative Support

Jakson Education System will keep up, refresh and run a far reaching and all-around incorporated website. JES will provide comprehensive & coordinated program to each franchisee to charge gathering purposes.

Quality Assurance

JES Quality Assurance Department (QAS) will do occasional Monitoring and Evaluation (M&E) visits so as to help and to guarantee adjustment to Jakson Education System quality and gauges.
The M&E procedure will recognize zones that should be reinforced and enhanced in this way realigning the school on the way of perfection. Institutionalized archives would be utilized in all establishment schools to guarantee consistence to our acclaimed norms.

IT Support

Information Technology is necessary requirement in modern world. Successful decisions are taken on reports from data and reports of IT Softwares. JES will provide fully functional ERP software installed in the school premises for Financials, HR, Inventory, Payables, Receivables and Cash Handling Module. Our experts will provide necessary on-hand training to management and employees in this regard.
